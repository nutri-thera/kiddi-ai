## Our Mission

At NutriThera AI LLC, we believe that a diagnosis is not a destiny. With the right nutritional guidance, patients can slow CKD progression and protect kidney function — one meal at a time.

KiDDiAI was created to give CKD patients and their caregivers a tool that's clinically grounded, personally tailored, and simple to use every day.

## The Team

KiDDiAI is built by a nephrologist from UT Southwestern Medical Center and a computer scientist specializing in AI — combining clinical expertise with modern technology to serve CKD patients.

## Clinical Foundation

KiDDiAI's nutrient targets and the Renal Balance Score are informed by:

- [KDOQI Clinical Practice Guidelines for Nutrition in CKD](https://www.kidney.org/professionals/guidelines)
- [KDIGO 2024 CKD Guidelines](https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/)
- [NKF Kidney Disease Education Resources](https://www.kidney.org/kidneydisease)
