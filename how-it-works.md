Managing a CKD diet is complicated. You're tracking protein, sodium, potassium, phosphorus, and fluid — all with different limits depending on your CKD stage, whether you're on dialysis, and whether you have other conditions like CHF.

KiDDiAI simplifies this into a single daily score: the **Renal Balance Score (RBS)**.

## What is the Renal Balance Score?

The RBS is a **0–100 daily score** that measures how well your food intake aligns with your kidney health goals. It's computed across five nutritional pillars:

- **Protein** — Too much accelerates kidney decline; too little causes malnutrition
- **Sodium** — High sodium raises blood pressure and worsens kidney disease
- **Potassium** — Dangerous at high levels for CKD Stage 4–5 and dialysis patients
- **Phosphorus** — Excess phosphorus harms bones and the cardiovascular system
- **Fluid** — Critical for dialysis patients who can't excrete excess fluid

Each pillar is scored independently, then combined with weights tailored to your profile.

## Four Scoring Profiles

KiDDiAI doesn't use a single formula for everyone. Your profile is selected based on your CKD stage and treatment status:

| Profile | Who It's For |
|---|---|
| **Standard CKD** | Stage 3–5, not on dialysis |
| **Dialysis** | Hemodialysis or peritoneal dialysis patients |
| **Fluid-Restricted** | CHF or edema, not on dialysis |
| **Early Stage** | Stage 0–3a (potassium/phosphorus weighted lower) |

This matters because, for example, a Stage 3 patient and a dialysis patient have very different potassium and protein targets.

## What the Score Means

| Score | Status | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| 85–100 | Optimal | Excellent dietary alignment |
| 70–84 | Stable | Good, with minor areas to improve |
| 50–69 | Caution | Notable imbalances to address |
| 0–49 | Critical | Significant dietary concerns |

## How to Use It

1. **Log your food** — Search the food database, scan barcodes, or photograph meals
2. **Check your RBS** — Your daily score updates in real time on the dashboard
3. **Review each pillar** — See exactly which nutrients are in or out of range
4. **Get tips** — KiDDiAI surfaces targeted advice for your lowest-scoring pillars

## Personalized to Your Limits

If your nephrologist or dietitian has prescribed specific nutrient limits, you can enter them directly in your profile. KiDDiAI will use those instead of the stage-based defaults.
